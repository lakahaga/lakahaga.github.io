- Successfully deployed in SK Telecom internal systems for real-world use.
- Achieved 1st place in the SKT AI Fellowship program.
- Published and presented at the Korea Institute of Information and Communication Engineering (KICS) Conference, Jeju (2022).
  Yerin Choi, Yeen-heui Yeen, and Dong-Geun Kim (2022). Dialogue System for User Customized Lecture Recommendation. Proceedings of the KICS Fall Conference.
  
### My Contribution
- Led data collection and augmentation for task-specific TOD dialogues
- Adapted generic task-oriented dialogue structures to the lecture recommendation domain via controlled style transfer
- Developed and integrated the full chatbot pipeline, from intent handling to response generation and deployment
